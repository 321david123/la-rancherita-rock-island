Who We Are

A family kitchen,
cooking the real thing.

La Rancherita is family-owned and operated — a neighborhood taqueria where the recipes come straight from home. We cook beans and rice, enchiladas, stuffed chiles and shrimp made to order, from the breakfast plate at 8 in the morning to dinner at night.

Locals know us for authentic street tacos, lengua and gorditas — not mass-market Tex-Mex. The hand-painted Bienvenidos storefront, the complimentary chips and salsa, and a kitchen that doesn't cut corners set a warm, welcoming tone. We're cash only, with an ATM on-site.
